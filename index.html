Our City/Community

Having nearly doubled in population in the last 10 years, Blue Ridge - an area of Northern Greenville County in South Carolina - has become a community ripe with Gospel potential. While the community has retained much of its rural feel, its landscape is now dotted with subdivisions and neighborhoods filled with thousands of people who only just recently have come to call Blue Ridge home. Most of these families work and play in places like Greenville and Greer, but Blue Ridge is the quiet oasis they have chosen to call home. Our call is engaging our friends and neighbors with the Good News of Jesus Christ.

Our Vision and Mission
